Questions you might have

Short answers to what we hear most often.

Does Bumle Bjørn cost anything?

You can download for free and use 2 categories (Animals and Colors and Shapes) plus 3 game modes. Bumle+ unlocks all 21 categories, all 7 game modes, unlimited voice recordings and voice-package sharing. Bumle+ is a one-time payment, not a subscription.

What is an AAC app, and is Bumle one?

AAC stands for Augmentative and Alternative Communication, and is for children without spoken language. Bumle is not an AAC app. Bumle helps children learn words and pronunciation, and is especially useful for parents with a dialect or speech therapists working on articulation.

How does voice-package sharing work?

You record audio in the app, tap «Share package», and get one file (.bumlepack). Send the file via AirDrop, iMessage, email or save it in Files. The recipient taps the file, and the recordings are imported into their Bumle. All sharing happens through the system's standard channels. Nothing goes through our servers.

Where are recordings and photos stored?

Only locally on your iPhone, encrypted with a device-specific key. Bumle Bjørn never sends recordings to our or third-party servers. When you explicitly share a voice package, it goes through your own email, iMessage or AirDrop.

Are there ads or tracking?

No. No ads, no third-party SDKs, no tracking. Bumle is approved for the App Store Kids Category, which requires strict compliance with children's privacy.

Which languages are supported?

9 languages: Norwegian (Bokmål), Danish, Swedish, Finnish, English, German, French, Spanish and Dutch. The whole app is translated, not just menus.

What age is the app for?

1 to 8 years. Younger children use simple modes like «Listen and find» and «Explore», while older children can use memory, counting and «Find in the picture». Difficulty adapts automatically.

How does parental control work?

Settings are gated behind a math problem (Kids Category requirement) and an optional PIN or Face ID. Screen-time limits, daily goals and weekly progress reports.
